About GEO-SOP

A practical GEO workspace for teams entering the AI answer era.

GEO-SOP focuses on one concrete problem: when customers ask AI tools about your category, does your brand appear, is it described accurately, and which sources shape the answer?

The product combines desktop browser collection, cloud account sync, screenshot evidence, citation source analysis, and AI-assisted next actions so brand, SEO, content, and growth teams can make decisions from observable data.

Built around evidence Every collection can keep answers, screenshots, platform state, references, and exports for later review.
Desktop first, cloud synced Sensitive platform login and browser collection stay on the user's computer, while cloud accounts keep tasks and dashboards connected.
Made for GEO workflows The workflow is designed for brand visibility, AI answer quality, source analysis, content planning, and recurring reporting.
What this GEO tool measures

Track how answer engines mention, rank, and cite your brand.

Use GEO-SOP to run repeatable prompts, capture evidence, compare AI answers, export local reports, and review whether your brand appears in AI search results for target topics.

AI answer monitoring Check ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity and Google AI responses for brand mentions, competitors, citations, and missing facts.

Security Notice

Unsigned builds may trigger system warnings.

This release is locally signed but not yet Apple-notarized or Windows code-signed, so macOS or Windows may warn that the publisher cannot be verified.

macOS Gatekeeper

First drag GEO-SOP into Applications. Then right-click the app and choose Open, or remove quarantine from the installed app.

xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine
"/Applications/GEO-SOP.app"

Production fix: Apple Developer ID signing + notarization.

Windows SmartScreen

Testing workaround: click More info, then choose Run anyway when the warning appears.

Production fix: OV/EV code signing certificate + signed installer.

GEO tools FAQ

Questions people ask before using a GEO tool.

What is a GEO tool?

A GEO tool helps teams monitor how generative engines and AI search systems answer questions about brands, categories, competitors, and buying decisions.

Does GEO-SOP replace Google SEO tools?

No. Use it beside Google Search Console, crawlers, rank trackers, and analytics. GEO-SOP focuses on AI answers, screenshots, references, and local evidence collection.

Which GEO keywords should I test?

Start with buyer questions, category comparisons, brand alternatives, local market prompts, and problem-solution queries that your customers may ask ChatGPT or other AI tools.
